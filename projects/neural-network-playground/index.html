VISUALIZATION

A neural network works by adding functions together. A neuron is simply a function which is added
together with other functions to form another function. In the output visualization, the y-axis of the
green lines show the output of every "hidden" neuron, and the y-axis on the black line shows the
(weighted) sum of those neurons (which is the output of the neural network).
