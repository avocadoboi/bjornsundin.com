So, what's Pomland?
Is it some kind of ice cream?
Well, Pomland is an RPG about a child
who escapes war, ending up on the
big island that is called Pomland.
The child then starts to explore
the island and finds interesting
stuff.

The game is inspired by games such
as Banjo-Kazooie, The Mario Series,
Undertale, Donkey Kong Country,
Crash Bandicoot and Earthbound.
